About Our Luminex App

Easily manage all your contacts in one place. Keep your connections organized, sync across devices, and enjoy a modern, user-friendly experience.

This contact application provides a seamless and flexible way for users to manage their contacts. You can log in through multiple methods, including Google, GitHub, or manual sign-up, making access convenient and secure. Once logged in, users can easily add new contacts, view existing ones, edit contact information, and delete entries as needed. The app also allows you to upload contact pictures directly to the cloud for safe storage and quick access. Additionally, you can export your entire contact list as an Excel file for easy data sharing or backup. With support for both dark and light modes, the interface adapts to your preferences, offering a sleek and modern design that is both free and easy to use. Whether you’re managing personal or professional contacts, this app simplifies the process, making it an essential tool for organizing your connections efficiently.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my contacts?

You can sync your contacts across devices by connecting your account to the cloud service in the settings.

Is my contact data secure?

Yes, all your data is encrypted and securely stored to ensure privacy.

Can I upload pictures for my contacts?

Absolutely! You can upload images for each contact, which will be stored securely in the cloud.

How do I export my contacts?

You can export your contacts as an Excel file through the settings menu. Just select the export option and follow the prompts.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page to reset it securely.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes, you can access your contacts offline, but syncing will require an internet connection to update changes.

What platforms is the app available on?

The app is available on both iOS and Android devices, as well as a web version for desktop users.

Do I need an account to use the app?

Yes, creating an account is necessary to access all features, including cloud syncing and backups.

Is there a limit to the number of contacts I can save?

No, you can save an unlimited number of contacts in your account.

Can I delete a contact after adding it?

Yes, you can easily delete any contact from your list at any time.
